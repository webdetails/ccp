Interactive BioMe Demo

Harnessing fitness device data to deliver high-value analytics to healthcare institutions.

Background

BioMe is a fictitious company that provides health and fitness monitoring devices to consumers to improve their well-being.
In order to reach new markets and monetize their existing data assets, BioMe decided to aggregate and repackage its extensive biometric sensor data in order to deliver a value-added analytics service to research institutions and healthcare companies.
In this application, which is driven by the Pentaho platform, analysts and researchers can both view high-level health trends by country and select their own custom data sets for drill-down analysis and visualization.

Main View

This is an intuitive analytic window into world heart rate trends. Take a quick tour to learn more.

  1. Mouse over the Avg. Heart Rate Time Analysis chart to see how each year compared to the long term average.
  2. Scroll down to the country cards to understand how country average heart rates compare to each other, represented by the size of each circle.
  3. Click on the countries you’d like to analyze further as part of a custom data set for ad hoc analysis and visualization.

Custom Dataset

Want to dive deeper into the biometric data? Create a custom data set on the fly.

  1. Click “Show Data Set Request” at the bottom of the page.
  2. Check the boxes to select the countries, biometric ranges, demographic categories, and time frames of interest.
  3. Hit "Go to Analyzer" to explore your data. If your data set isn’t ready yet, you can hit "Processing Requests" to check on the progress.

In-Depth Interactive Analysis

Explore your data through three distinct views – then customize as you like.

  1. The first view is ”Avg. Heart Rate”, represented by the blue grid icon. Click on the rectangular icons above ’No Filters’ to drag more metrics into your analysis.
  2. Click the middle line chart icon for ”Avg. Heart Rate Time Analysis”. You can adjust the year being viewed with the ”Year of Analysis” slider at the upper right.
  3. Click the rightmost scatter icon for “Heart Rate vs. Systolic Blood Pressure”, which displays individual biometric results. Try dragging a square shape over a subset of the data points.

Technology

  • Out of the box
  • Customizations
  • Pentaho Data Integration to design and automate the data set request process behind the scenes and streamline data refinement.
  • Pentaho Analyzer for pivot-style ad hoc analysis and visualization of data.
  • Pentaho CTools for the custom dashboard in the main view as well as the data request form.
  • Pentaho components were embedded into a 3rd party application that leverages Angular JS for dynamic views.
  • Pentaho’s JavaScript APIs were leveraged to embed Analyzer in the 3rd party application and power pre-populated chart buttons and timeframe controls.
  • RequireJS, a JavaScript file and module loader, was leveraged in embedding the Ctools dashboard into this application.
  • The BioMe embedded dashboards were built with Bootstrap, a responsive front-end development framework.
